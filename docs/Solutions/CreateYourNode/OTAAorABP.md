# OTAA or ABP?

## OTAA Over-the-Air-Activation (default)
* Most secure
* Dynamic DevAddr

Const:
* AppEUI
* AppKey

## ABP Activation by Personalisation
* Less secure
* Simpler, becouse skip join procedure

Const:
* DevADDR
* NwkSKey
* AppSKey
